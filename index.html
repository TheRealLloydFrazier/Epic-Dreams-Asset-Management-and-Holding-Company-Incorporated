About

Epic Dreams Asset Management and Holding Company, Incorporated (the "Company") oversees and allocates capital across a growing family of businesses. We focus on durable value creation, operational excellence, and principled governance.

The Company is currently the sole parent of two wholly owned subsidiaries and is governed by a Board of Directors with deep experience across technology, finance, and operations.

Governance & Shareholding

The Company is privately held and founder‑controlled. All issued shares are held by the Founders; remaining authorized shares are unissued and reserved. Any future issuances require Board approval. Eligibility for ownership is limited to people directly related to the Founders; spouses are excluded.

Detailed cap table and corporate resolutions are available to qualified lenders and investors upon request.
