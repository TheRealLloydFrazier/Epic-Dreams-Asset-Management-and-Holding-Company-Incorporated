About Lloyd Frazier

Lloyd Frazier brings a unique blend of entrepreneurial vision and artistic creativity to Epic Dreams Records. As both the CEO of Epic Dreams Entertainment and a recording artist, Lloyd creates music that reflects deep storytelling, innovative soundscapes, and emotional authenticity.

His musical journey spans multiple genres, incorporating elements that push boundaries while maintaining a connection to timeless songwriting traditions. Each release showcases his evolution as an artist and his commitment to creating meaningful musical experiences.

About Kelly Layton

Kelly Layton's distinctive voice and artistic vision make her a standout talent on the Epic Dreams Records roster. Her music captures authentic emotions and delivers powerful performances that resonate with audiences worldwide.

With a vocal style that combines power and vulnerability, Kelly creates songs that speak to the human experience. Her dedication to her craft and unique perspective bring fresh energy to every project, establishing her as a vital voice in contemporary music.
